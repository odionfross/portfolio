I am currently working remotely in Vancouver as a product designer for a health tech company in Toronto, meshMD, that I have had a full-time work relationship with since I was in Toronto. Having worked for meshMD for 2.5 years taught me how to balance between meeting business goals, deeply caring about users, and collaborating with coworkers.

How Did I Get Here?

My interest in UX/UI design started from when I took the Multimedia Design and Development program at Humber College, where I studied not only UX/UI but also front-end development for two years. My extensive knowledge in coding enables me to speak better with developers, understand technical constraints even before designing, and articulate my design intention better.
